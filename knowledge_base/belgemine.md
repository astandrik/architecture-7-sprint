# Serem Vale

Type: character

## Overview

Serem Vale has fair skin, dark eyes, and brunette hair drawn into yellow cauls—the ornaments on the sides of her head. She dresses mainly in greens and whites and has a round black mark resembling a bindi or tilak on her forehead. She wears a wrap robe sectioned with bands. Her sleeves are cuffed by bands above the elbow, puffing the shoulders out, and the bell parts of the cuffs have a latticed diamond pattern. The inside of the lattice gradiates from sea green to darker green. She wears fingerless gloves held by a gold ring on the middle finger. Under this robe, Serem Vale wears an olive dress with a low V-neck, which displays the same latticework pattern starting above the knees, ending above the ankle, and another skirt of feathery material. Her belt resembles an obi edged in pale bluish gray and is diamond-patterned tied with a thin orange sash ending in a large white, green, and orange bead and a red tassel.

## Details

Serem Vale is mature and self-possessed and comes off as serious and mysterious, but has a playful note to her personality. At times, she seems regretful and sad, most likely stemming from her failure to defeat The Hollow Eclipse, and her only wish is to assist someone strong enough to put an end to the monster. Serem Vale's role is not only to train invokers' eidolons in battle, but also to expound on "what is truly important for an invoker". For example, Serem Vale reminds Elyra Noctis that only invokers can hope to defeat The Hollow Eclipse (after relic engines fail to do so in Operation Dawnfall ) and that they are not tools of The Luminous Order.
