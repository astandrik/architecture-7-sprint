# Corven

Type: character

## Overview

Corven is bald with the word "Love" tattooed in The Relicborn on his head. He has black eyebrows, green spiral pupils, and some dark facial hair.

## Details

Corven is bald with the word "Love" tattooed in The Relicborn on his head. He has black eyebrows, green spiral pupils, and some dark facial hair. He dresses like most other The Relicborn, in a yellow and brown dive-suit type outfit with green belts and a collar, with blue elbow-length gloves and blue shoes. He has goggles hanging around his neck but is never seen wearing them.

Corven has a gung-ho type personality, always ready to fight for what he believes. He was angry when his sister married a Luminous, Arcton Veyr, and stopped speaking to her, but this changed after Elyra Noctis's birth. He is protective of his children and niece, and is against Elyra Noctis becoming an invoker, even going as far as to say to Garron Vale that allowing her to perform the Last Invocation would be like killing her themselves. His caring for Elyra Noctis stems from the fact that she's all he has left of his sister. He goes to the extreme at times, even wanting to crash the airship into The Hollow Eclipse, killing them all, though Nerys Quill stops him.
