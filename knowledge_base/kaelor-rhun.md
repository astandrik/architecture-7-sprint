# Kaelor Rhun

Type: character

## Overview

Kaelor, while taller than humans, is smaller than the average Rhunari and has a broken horn. He is a fierce protector of Elyra Noctis and very loyal to her. He is mistrusting of others at first and rarely speaks. He dislikes others trying to solve his problems for him.

## Details

Kaelor has blue fur and white hair and a beard. He is leonine, tall, muscular, and has a tail. He has a broken horn on his forehead, causing him great shame as a Rhunari. His ears are pierced and his hair is held back in a ponytail and braided on the sides. Due to his fur, Kaelor doesn't need to wear clothes for warmth. He wears leather straps that hold in place his arm guards and his chest piece that has a skull motif. He wears ankle guards and a sash tied around his waist with a red belt. His shoulder guards have tufts of white feathers or fur.
