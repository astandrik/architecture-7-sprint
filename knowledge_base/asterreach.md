# Asterreach

Type: location

## Overview

The camp site is where all invokers and oathsworn rest before they enter the dome and complete their pilgrimage.

## Details

The Road to Asterreach is lined with the ruins of fallen buildings and roads. The entire area is covered in ember motes.

The Asterreach Dome is the ruins of the former temple and government seat of Asterreach. Its architecture resembles the skyball stadium in Dream Asterreach.
