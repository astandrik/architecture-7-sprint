# The Hollow Eclipse

Type: concept

## Overview

The Hollow Eclipse is preserved in the archive as a concept woven into Elyndran history, ritual memory, and the synthetic world model prepared for retrieval.

## Details

The Hollow Eclipse has a whale-like body that it moves with a pair of clawed arms, as well as hind legs resembling pectoral fins for movement in water. It has a long tail and its body is encased in scales it can shed and turn into smaller monsters known as Eclipse shards. It carries part of a city, presumed to be a part of Asterreach, on its body close to the back of the head. The Hollow Eclipse's final form has feathery wing-like protrusions that are purple at the tips.

The Hollow Eclipse can create smaller creatures, Eclipseborn, from its outer layer. Coming in a variety of shapes and levels of power, the Eclipseborn cause trouble for any person that crosses them until The Hollow Eclipse arrives and reabsorbs them.
