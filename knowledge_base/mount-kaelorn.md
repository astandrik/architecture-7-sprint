# Mount Kaelorn

Type: location

## Overview

The snow-covered, towering Mount Kaelorn is considered sacred land. Before the Endless Truce only invokers and their oathsworn were permitted to pass beyond the mountain gate as the final test of the invoker's resolve before entering the Asterreach Ruins.

## Details

The Floating Ruins are situated at the peak of Mount Kaelorn. The peak used to be perpetually shrouded in fog, but after the Dreambound Scar became dormant the fog lifted revealing the ruins at the top.

Torren Kaid must hit a target with his skyball to open a new path within the caves.

Reward: Stonehewn Garment Grid, Syndicate Uniform.

Garik, in his hatred of the Sylvan Court, has decided to launch an attack! Meet him on the summit of Mount Kaelorn and stop him - by force, if that's what it takes.
