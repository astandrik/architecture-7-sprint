## Details

The Sylvan Court have a religious culture, which causes them to come across as arrogant. Their role as oathsworn of the Veilward have led to them looking down upon other races. The Sylvan Court were converted to the Luminous Order religion under their leader, Vhalric, who was later appointed a High Preceptor of The Luminous Order. Despite their strict religious culture, they are generous and welcoming to guests.
