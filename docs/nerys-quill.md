# Nerys Quill

Type: character

## Overview

Nerys Quill is preserved in the archive as a character woven into Elyndran history, ritual memory, and the synthetic world model prepared for retrieval.

## Details

Nerys Quill is a slender and athletic teenage girl who has medium-length blonde hair in a high ponytail with two braids hanging down, decorated with orange feathers. Two shorter sections of hair part to her left and frame her face. A pair of bobby pins, one blue and one green, crisscross the left side. She also has the Relicborn trademark green eyes with swirled pupils.

Nerys Quill is also one of the three characters that can fight underwater, along with Caelan Veyr and Torren Kaid.
