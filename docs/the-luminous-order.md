## Details

Temples of The Luminous Order are found throughout Elyndra. At the heart of each temple lies a Chamber of the Dreambound, a room that contains a dreambound —a statue that houses a willingly-given human soul. Through the soul bonding to an invoker mighty creatures called eidolons may be made manifest.

There are five official temples of The Luminous Order, each with their own dreambound:

Veylspire – Home to the dreambound Starfang. Also the central temple of The Luminous Order.

Asterreach – At the Asterreach Dome the invoker can acquire the Crown Eidolon.
