# Caelan Veyr

Type: character

## Overview

He is a rising star skyball player from Asterreach and the son of major skyball star Darius Veyr. After the gargantuan monster known as The Hollow Eclipse attacks his hometown, Caelan Veyr is transported to the world of Elyndra. Lost, confused, and seemingly out of place, Caelan Veyr meets a newly fledged invoker named Elyra Noctis and her oathsworn. Elyra Noctis is to set out on a pilgrimage to put an end to The Hollow Eclipse and, by joining Elyra Noctis on her pilgrimage, Caelan Veyr hopes he will find his way home. Caelan Veyr is upbeat and initially childish and naive to the world of Elyndra. He is also selfless and will do what he believes is right, even if he doesn't think it through first. Throughout the pilgrimage, he matures and becomes more level-headed and grounded.

## Details

To Caelan Veyr's dismay, he has similarities to his father, who tended to ignore responsibility, take things easy, and never worry about the present situation instead of leaving things for future. Caelan Veyr's resemblance to his father is pointed out by Garron Vale, who says that Caelan Veyr is "the one running away". A lot of Caelan Veyr's personality is shaped by the comments that he used to receive from his father and how he grew up to resent him. Despite having been verbally offensive, at heart, Darius Veyr was proud of his son and his resolve. Caelan Veyr confronts his childhood and his relationship with his father when living out his new life in Elyndra and matures from a self-centered and outspoken athlete to a selfless hero.
