## Details

Torren Kaid depends on The Luminous Order's religion and guidance, making him socially conservative and, thus, is intentionally kept in the dark about Elyra Noctis's half-The Relicborn heritage. Because of his high value in his religion, Torren Kaid is reluctant to participate in anything considered forbidden by its teachings. Traveling Elyndra with Elyra Noctis's party broadens his worldview, he begins to see the hypocrisy behind his previous actions, and he apologizes to Corven and the other The Relicborn for his ignorance.

Torren Kaid has a big heart and frequently attempts to cheer the party up, though he is not always successful. He tends to goof around with the younger members of the group, much to the annoyance of Selene Marr, who berates him for setting a bad example. Though they bicker, Torren Kaid is close to her and affectionately calls her "Lu".
