## Details

When Caelan Veyr's party visits the Sisters of the Eidolons' Chamber of the Dreambound the second time, their dreambound will appear before them. In contrast to their eidolon appearance, the dreambound are roughly the same height and body type. Their dreambound statue resembles how they look as eidolons only with blonde hair.

Unlike other eidolons, the Sisters of the Eidolons consist of three separate entities. Their specific commands cannot be controlled; Elyra Noctis can only suggest actions for them to take by shouting commands such as "Fight!", "Help Each Other!", "Go, Go, Go!", but the resulting effect under each heading is random, and the sisters may randomly choose "taking a break...", which wastes a turn. This is compensated for by the Sisters of the Eidolons being extremely powerful and able to break the 9,999 damage limit from the start. Because the sisters are not directly controllable, they cannot use Shield or Boost.

Cindy's special attack, Camisade, deals physical damage to an enemy, and Sandy's special attack, Razzia, functions similarly. Mindy's special attack, Passado, deals 15 hits of physical damage to a single target, making it one of the most potentially powerful attacks in the game.

Motivation is the 0–100 numerical measure of the relationship between Elyra Noctis and each of the sisters. At the start of battle, each sister's Motivation is 50. Motivation increases whenever a sister performs an action, and decreases with "Taking a break..." Motivation affects which action the sisters choose to take, but because the sisters are more inclined to act than "take a break", generally it means they will use their better attacks more the longer they stay in the battlefield. "Taking a break..." happens if none of the other attacks available for the chosen command trigger. The actions often considered the best yield more Motivation as well, such as Auto-Life, Ultima and Delta Attack.
