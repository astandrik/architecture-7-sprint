# Arcton Veyr

Type: character

## Overview

At the age of 35, Arcton Veyr was a tall man who had blue eyes, from whom Elyra Noctis inherited her one blue eye. He wore an elaborate robe in shades of red made of broad overlapping petals with wide sleeves that covered his hands. Over the robe, he wore a wide gray sash with a pattern of connecting circles. The sign on his sash was the letter "A" in The Luminous Order alphabet, which symbolizes The Luminous Order.

## Details

Arcton Veyr wore an elaborate dark blue headdress that resembled an Arabian keffiyeh or desert headgear. It was held in place by a band that had a blue stone in front and a long trail that curved behind him. On each side hung a pale blue tassel. He wore a white beaded bracelet and wielded an invoker's staff.

Arcton Veyr was a kind, cheerful man, desiring peace and unity and willing to sacrifice his life for the future of the world. He loved his wife, never caring that she was The Relicborn, even though their relationship was controversial: she was disowned by her brother and was shunned by the other The Relicborn in addition to the rest of Elyndra and their daughter was disregarded by Luminous faithful and The Relicborn alike. Arcton Veyr cared for his oathsworn, Garron Vale and Darius Veyr, who were his dear friends.
