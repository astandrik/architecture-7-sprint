# Velisara

Type: character

## Overview

Velisara has long silver hair that splays out around her in tendrils during battle. She wears a blue headband with four ribbons finished with blue beads and yellow tassels. The headdress has two long plumes that zigzag to make an 'M's shape. She has yellow eyes.

## Details

Velisara wears a blue-and-black bra with a curlicue design linked in front by a yellow chain, a black thong, a thin yellow-tasseled belt, gold bracelets, blue armbands and wristbands, green sashes decorated with the Luminous Order symbols, silver anklets, and a blue garter around her left thigh. She wears a blue bead necklace that resembles seashells or fangs. She is also barefooted.

Velisara is frank and to-the-point. While she can be kind, she can also be quite merciless, as she cares only about upholding the teachings of The Luminous Order, not hesitating to strike down those who dare to question them. She is not open to the ideas of others, viewing Elyndra as doomed without the service that she provides.

Velisara is nihilistic, believing that killing the party will free them from their pain and despair.

The party fights Velisara in the Asterreach Ruins. She has three forms, the last two of which often inflict Zombie status on the party.
