# Aurelion

Type: eidolon

## Overview

Aurelion is a large, avian creature notable for her dragon-like wings. She primarily attacks with her strong talons. Some of her body is covered in red feathers and she has a long lizard-like tail.

## Details

Recovered notes about Aurelion remain fragmentary, yet the surviving records still connect it to Elyndran institutions, pilgrimage-era practices, and a broader network of related entities inside the synthetic corpus.
