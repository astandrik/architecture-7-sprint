# The Wardens of Dawn

Type: faction

## Overview

The Wardens of Dawn is preserved in the archive as a faction woven into Elyndran history, ritual memory, and the synthetic world model prepared for retrieval.

## Details

On Veyn, the Wardens of Dawn's Lodge serves as an inn for both The Wardens of Dawn and guests to the island. Caelan Veyr is offered to rest there, where he first learns of the Wardens of Dawn's plight to defeat The Hollow Eclipse.
