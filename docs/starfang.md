# Starfang

Type: eidolon

## Overview

Starfang is a large, black dragon with enormous red wings. He can stand or move in either a bipedal or quadrupedal fashion.

## Details

Starfang is obtained after completing the Sanctum of Ordeals in Veylspire Temple.
